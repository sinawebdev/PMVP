The Problem

Workers are paid in the dark.

Across Ghana, payroll runs are calculated by hand and payslips never make it to the people they belong to. We've seen it from the inside.

No visibility

Employees receive a lump-sum bank credit with zero insight into PAYE, SSNIT, overtime, or deductions. They can't question what they can't see.

Delivery breaks down

HR prints slips or sends them one-by-one over WhatsApp — slow, inconsistent, impossible to audit, and unworkable past twenty staff.

Data lives in chaos

Raw clock-in timestamps and salaries sit in fragile Excel files — no validation, no duplicate detection, no trail when numbers go wrong.

How It Works

One pipeline, end to end.

Raw hours go in. A calculated, delivered, and tracked payslip comes out — for every employee, on every payday.
